什么是抑郁症

 

抑郁症(重度抑郁症)是一种常见而严重的医学疾病，会对你的感觉、思维方式和行为方式产生负面影响。幸运的是，抑郁症也是可以治疗的。抑郁会导致悲伤和/或对你曾经喜欢的活动失去兴趣，还会导致各种情绪和身体问题，并会降低你在工作和家庭中的能力。 Depression (major depressive disorder) is a common and serious medical illness that negatively affects how you feel, the way you think and how you act. Fortunately, it is also treatable. Depression causes feelings of sadness and/or a loss of interest in activities you once enjoyed. 抑 郁 科 普更多 》
  • 我们总是把抑郁情绪和抑郁症混为一谈。情绪低落并不等于抑郁症，它们一般持续时间很短，程度很轻，可以自动缓解或者通过自身调节而康复。抑郁症则是每天情绪低落、快感缺乏、兴趣丧失，且至少持续两周以上。只有达到一定的程度和频度才能够被诊断为抑郁症。
  • We sometimes confuse depression with sadness. Sadness is a healthy emotion to feel when faced with a difficult situation and usually we cope and soon recover without treatment.Depression can be debilitating and require additional support. A major depressive disorder lasts for at least two weeks and affects a person’s ability to work, carry out usual daily activities and have satisfying personal relationships.
