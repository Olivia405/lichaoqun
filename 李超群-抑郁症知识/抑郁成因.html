生 物 学 差 异
抑郁症患者似乎在其大脑中具有生理变化。这些变化的意义尚不确定，但可能最终有助于查明病因。 Biochemistry: Differences in certain chemicals in the brain may contribute to symptoms of depression.People with depression seem to have physical changes in their brains. The significance of these changes is uncertain, but may eventually help pinpoint the cause.
脑 化 学 物 质
神经递质是天然存在的脑化学物质，可能在抑郁症中发挥作用。最近研究表明，这些神经递质的功能和作用改变以及它们如何与维持情绪稳定的神经回路相互作用，可能在抑郁症及其治疗中发挥重大作用。 Recent research suggests that changes in the function and action of these neurotransmitters and how they interact with neural circuits that maintain emotional stability may play a significant role in depression and its treatment.
激 素 分 泌
身体激素平衡的变化可能与引起或引发抑郁症。激素变化可能随怀孕、在分娩后（产后）几周或几个月期间以及因甲状腺问题、绝经或许多其他病症产生。Changes in the body's hormonal balance may be associated with causing or triggering depression.
遗 传 因 素
在血亲也有这种状况的人中，抑郁症更为常见。研究人员正在试图找到可能与引起抑郁症的有关基因。Depression can run in families. For example, if one identical twin has depression, the other has a 70 percent chance of having the illness sometime in life.
环 境 因 素
持续遭受暴力、忽视、虐待或贫困可能会使一些人更容易患抑郁症。Continuous exposure to violence, neglect, abuse or poverty may make some people more vulnerable to depression.
